border-radius: two radii to make elliptical corners.

For elliptical corners there are 2 parameters to the border radius declaration. That is: param 1: The horizontal radius. param 2: the vertical radius.

The values before the / are styles for horizontal radius and those after are values for the vertical radius styles:

border-radius: 30%;
border-radius: 30% 30%;
border-radius: 30% / 10%;
